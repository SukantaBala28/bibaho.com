What is Lingo121?

Lingo121 is a website that helps language learners find private tutors -- like yourself -- in cities across Canada.

How does it work for language learners?

Language learners enter their city and the language they’re studying, and then review the profiles of tutors available. They can message you, book a session, and pay for the lesson through the site. Afterward, they can review their experience.

How does it work for tutors?

Potential tutors apply to have their services listed on the site. If the application is approved, the tutor is invited to customize a profile. Once the profile is active, it will turn up in relevant search results. Learners can then message the tutor, book their services, and pay for their lessons all through the site.

What cities are available?

We are currently accepting tutor applications in all Canadian cities. We will expand beyond Canada in the coming year.

What languages are available?

We are currently accepting tutor applications for any teachable language, however, we will closely monitor and control the number of tutors teaching any given language relative to the number of learners searching for it. This is to ensure there are enough students to make it worth your while.

Does my application guarantee my profile will be listed?

NO. Unlike some sites, westrictly control the quality of our tutors so our learners can count on high standards of language instruction. We will review the information you submit and determine whether you are eligible using a variety of assessment criteria. There's no one thing that makes you eligible (eg. a TESOL certification). Rather we look at the whole of your application to determine your suitability. If we think you’re suitable, but we are full in that particular language, we may offer you a position on our waiting list.

Where do the lessons happen?

Although we are a technology company, we believe in the power of dialogic, in-person teaching, so tutoring sessions are in "the real world". On your profile you will list locations where you feel comfortable offering the class (e.g. "my home", "Cafe", "open to suggestions" etc).

What’s the minimum age to use the site?

18; people under the age of 18 must have a parent or legal guardian communicate and arrange sessions on their behalf.

Can learners contact the tutors before booking a session?

Yes! Learners can send you a message through your profile to ask questions, e.g. “Do you have experience teaching TOEFL?”.

Can learners book their sessions through the site?

Yes! Once learners are sure of the tutor they’d like to study with, they can make a booking a request through the profile. The tutor has 24 hours to respond and can either (i) accept the request as is, (ii) suggest modifications to the request (e.g. time or location), or (iii) reject the request.

Can learners pay for their lessons through the site?

Yes! Once learners have successfully booked a lesson, they will be asked to pay for it. Payments can be made with a credit card or a PayPal account.

How much does the site charge?

Tutors set their hourly rate and the site adds $5. The combined total is the amount that appears on the tutors’ profiles.

What does the site do with the money collected?

First we pay HST and payment processing fees. The remainder is used to maintain the site and, most importantly, get more learners for you.

Are tutors employees of the site?

No, tutors are independent contractors and work “for themselves”. As a tutor, you are personally responsible for declaring any income you make to relevant government authorities.

How do the tutors get paid?

Once your application is approved, we will ask for your banking information. At the end of every month, you’ll receive an e-mail summarizing your activity for the month, and a direct deposit of your fees will be made to your bank account.

As a tutor, couldn’t I just stop using the site after the first meeting with a new student?

Yes, you could; but keep in mind the $5 fee is how we are able to get you more learners, so it’s in your own interest to keep your learners coming through the site.

How do learners review their tutors?

After the scheduled lesson time has passed, the learner will be invited to submit a review of their selected tutor. These are internally reviewed and then posted to the learner’s profile.

Can learners request a refund?

There is no "refund policy" per se; however, if a learner contacts us to express disappointment with the class and request a refund, we will contact you to let you know. As an independent contractor, it is up to you whether or not you want to refund all or part of the money. When making this decision, keep in mind the learner has the opportunity to leave a review on your page. If you believe learners are maliciously targeting you for financial gain, please contact us right away.
